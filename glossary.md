# Glossary

Term | Definition
-----|------------
BG   | Blood Glucose
BLE  | Bluetooth Low Energy
CGM  | Continuous Glucose Monitoring
LGS  | Low Glucose Suspend
PLGM | Predictive Low Glucose Management
SG   | Sensor Glucose: based on levels measured by a glucose sensor in the interstitial fluid, as opposed to _Blood Glucose_ which is measured using blood
TBR  | Temporary Basal Rate
TIR  | Time in Range
